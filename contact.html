Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and visiting our clinic

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond to WhatsApp messages within 2-4 hours during business hours, emails within 24 hours, and phone calls are answered immediately during operating hours.

Do I need an appointment to visit the clinic?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability and reduce waiting time. Emergency cases are always accommodated.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring any medical records, current medications list, insurance information, and a list of your health concerns and symptoms.

Do you offer consultations in languages other than English?

Yes, our practitioners speak English, Yoruba, and Hausa. We can also arrange interpreters for other languages with advance notice.

Can I get treatment advice over the phone?

We can provide general information over the phone, but specific treatment recommendations require an in-person or video consultation for safety and accuracy.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Contact us at least 24 hours in advance via WhatsApp, phone, or email to cancel or reschedule. This helps us accommodate other patients.
